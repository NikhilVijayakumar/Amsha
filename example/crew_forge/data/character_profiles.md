# Character Profiles

## Sparkle the Dragon
- **Description**: A small, glittering green dragon who loves to bake cookies but accidentally sets them on fire with his sneezes.
- **Personality**: Optimistic, clumsy, helpful, loves sweets.
- **Friends**: A wise old owl named Hoot and a fast squirrel named Nutty.

## Hoot the Owl
- **Description**: An ancient owl with glasses who knows everything about the forest.
- **Personality**: Wise, patient, slightly grumpy if woken up before sunset.

## Nutty the Squirrel
- **Description**: A hyperactive squirrel with a blue tail.
- **Personality**: Fast, erratic, loyal, forgetful.
